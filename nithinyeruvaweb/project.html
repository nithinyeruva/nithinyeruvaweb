Automated Programming and Testing Fixture for Electronics Manufacturing @Douglas Innovation

What?

  • Tasked to design and fabricate a device that programs and tests electronics in a factory environment
  • Given coordinates for precision gold pins to be in contact with the board

How?

  • Used Autodesk Inventor to design and CAM works to program CNC to create electronic board mounting due to the needs of precision
  • The main body which was 3D printed, went through various iterations to achieve peak efficiency and strength
  • Used soldering skills to attach gold contact pins and the following wires to the control box which programmed the board

Results

  • Manufactured many models to send to plant in Mexico
  • Attached clamps on both sides of the board so the electronic board would stay in constant contact with the gold pins
  • Placed programing controller at the bottom of the box to reduce the footprint of the device
